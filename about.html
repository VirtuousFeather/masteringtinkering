A little bit about myself



Hi there, I'll go by the alias 'Feather' on this page!

Like the title of this page suggests, I'll be using this webpage
as a means of virtually displaying things I've made over the course
of the CreaTe program!

To tell you a bit about the owner of this page (A.K.A. me :D):
I'm originally from the province of Noord-Brabant.
I like to spend my time being creative, playing videogames with
both old and new friends and listening to music when I really
need my me-time.
As for why I chose to join the CreaTe program, I like to both play around
with my creativity as well as my technological interests.
I like to figure out why and (most importantly) how things work
the way they do.

The CreaTe program is only available in Enschede as of now, so I decided
to move to Enschede and enjoy my time as a student here!
Even though moving to Enschede and joining the official UTwente Kick-In
have been quite stressful and chaotic at times, I've had a lot of fun in the last few days!
From the sports association workshops teaching you how to dance, to row or
to use self defense to meeting new students from all over the world,
it's been a fun ride so far!

Let's enjoy this year and all years to come!
